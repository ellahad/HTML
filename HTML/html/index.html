About Us

At EVANA Beauty, we believe that beauty is more than skin deep. Our journey began when our founder, Emily, struggled with sensitive skin and couldn't find products that truly worked. Determined to create a solution, she teamed up with a group of passionate experts in skincare and cosmetics.

Meet the Team

  • Emily (Founder & CEO): With a background in dermatology, Emily brings her scientific expertise to every product we create.
  • Sophia (Creative Director): An artist at heart, Sophia ensures that our packaging and branding reflect the beauty within.
  • Alex (Product Formulator): Alex's chemistry background ensures that our formulations are effective and safe.

Our Mission and Values

  • Quality: Every ingredient is carefully selected for its efficacy and safety.
  • Inclusivity: Beauty knows no boundaries, and neither do we. Our shades cater to all skin tones.
  • Sustainability: We're on a journey to reduce our environmental footprint.
